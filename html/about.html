Who we are

The Mediterranean Network (MEDNet4Music) for music is a newly established consortium of Music institutions and organizations. Its members represent a wide range of establishments operating professionally within the Mediterranean region comprising of music departments in public and private universities, Conservatoires, arts organizations and other professional enterprises.

MEDNet4Music is dedicated to supporting, advocating and promoting innovation in the field of Music taking place within the Mediterranean region. It particularly focuses on Music Performance, Composition, Music Education, Technology and Research, as well as interdisciplinary fields. It is committed to documenting, preserving, disseminating and advancing music activities of all facets and spectrums that represent the richness and complexity of the Mediterranean musical landscape.

MEDNet4Music is dedicated to identifying, sharing and preserving Mediterranean music of all aesthetics, genres and idioms with a strong focus and emphasis on classical, traditional and ethnic music. MEDNet4Music is very active in promoting artistic exchange with other regions, either through the application for European Union funding or other national and international schemes.

Activities and events take place within the wide general public domain through concerts, festivals, master classes and community outreach but also within more specialized forums such as lectures, publications, CD and DVD productions, symposiums and conferences.
